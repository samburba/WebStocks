About

SmileAi

SmileAI is a combonation project for Advanced Web Development and Artificial Intellegence by Sam Burba and Long Nguyen.

SmileAI's goal is to create a friendly enviroment users can trade virtual stocks on a simulated market. The goal is to encourage users to interact with each other to trade tips and knowledge on the stock market. And if users get stuck and can't make a decision on a good stock to buy, our AI advisor will help out.

Features

SmileAI's Features

Artificially Intellegent Advisor

We are developing and updating our advisor to give the best advice on when to buy or sell stock

Stylish Design

Even through the focus is on the stock market, we wanted to have a user friendly enviorment. Thanks to our head designer Long, we aren't sacrificing form for function

User Communication

We wanted to encourage users to communicate with one another. That is why we are improving our site to make commenting about stocks as easy as possible.

Leaderboard

While our website is aimed at learning, we added a leaderboard to encourage smart trading (and for bragging rights).

Free

Yes, it is free for everyone.

Faq

Questions and Answers.

Is this real money?

No. When you create an account, you are given $1000 virtual dollars to use to buy simulated stocks.

Can I "cash out" for real money?

No.

Do I actually own stock I invest in?

Not on this site.

How does the AI advisor work?

We use an algorithm to look at a stocks performance for the previous week. Judging by the analysis ran by the AI, it decides if it is a good time to buy or sell the stock.

Is the AI advisor alway accurate?

No.

Is the AI advisor ever accurate?

Yes.

Are you updating the AI to be more accurate?

Yes. The AI is always in constant development.
